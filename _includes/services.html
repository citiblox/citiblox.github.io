MODULAR

Precast pavers made from reinforced concrete enable faster maintenance and replacement. Blocks can be removed or replaced to change the function of the street.

DYNAMIC

LEDs can be activated when a street is being used to indicate different functions, and can be programmed to change colour depending on the environment.

ELECTRIC

Pavers generate their own energy to charge electric vehicles, and conductive concrete will melt snow and ice.

SUSTAINABLE

Dedicated landscape zones can be introduced to improve local air quality and absorb water runoff.
