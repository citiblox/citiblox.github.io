Be the first to walk along the street of the future…

Citiblox are a series of modular pavers can be replaced in a matter of minutes, allowing for a more adaptable street infrastructure and a space for urban experimentation.

Citiblox provides a platform for urban applications to collect data on the social and environmental characteristics of the local urban environment. These digital solutions can:

- Improve some quality-of-life indicators by 10–30%

- Shave the average commute by 15–20%

- Cut greenhouse gas emissions by 10–15%

Citiblox presents governments with an opportunity to adopt a powerful and cost-effective form of street infrastructure.
